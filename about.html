About Me

"Jack of All Trades"

I've worked in a number of trades from retail, construction, farm-hand, service, ministry, factory, maintenance, commercial cleaning, business ownership, marketing, landscaping, graphic design, photography, web design, to UX design.

I feel that I bring additional insight and skills due to my wide variety of work history and education. And if I don't know how to do it, I will learn how to quickly.

I strive to work in an environment and company that has a blend of work time and location flexibility, innovation, user-focus, employee empathy, self-direction, fun, and a determined "why".

As a cancer survivor I don't want to work just to work, instead I want to do work that has an impact on the customer/user to fulfill their goals, dreams, and needs; and feel the accomplishment of those goals.

Life & Hobbies

I'm a father, husband, and outdoorsman who enjoys running, hiking, camping, fishing, and hunting. In my "spare" time, I like to travel, do art, woodworking, gunsmithing, and restoration projects.
